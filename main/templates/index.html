OUR OBJECTIVES

The Ontario Association of Child Protection Lawyers started in Spring 2017 in Windsor, Ontario with a group of Family Lawyers who saw a need to increase the level of advocacy for children, families, and communities that were struggling in terms of Child Welfare.

The OACPL as an organization seeks to:

- increase the efficiency and level of professional expertise offered by Child Protection Lawyers in Ontario

- provide public and professional education and input from Recognized Child Protection Lawyers on Child Welfare Reform and Practice

- provide an organized body of Recognized Child Protection Lawyers that advocate politically and socially on Child Welfare Matters in Ontario

- encourage the highest standards of practice and support for children, families and communities affected by Child Protection Litigation.
